Description

Beef Curry Rice is a classic asian inspired dish.

Simple in preparation, complex in taste. The perfect bachelor's dish.

  • Rice
  • Package of Curry Powder
  • Cut of Skirt Steak

Steps

  1. Steam rice in rice cooker.
  2. Mix curry powder into pot on stovetop and prepare based on package instructions. Let simmer.
  3. Cut skirt steak into bite sized pieces and cook in a medium-high pan until brown and cooked to preference.
  4. Mix steak into simmering curry pot.
  5. Remove rice as needed into a bowl, cover with sauce/steak mix.
  6. Enjoy!
