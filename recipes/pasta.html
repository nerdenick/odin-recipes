Fettuccini Alfredo

Description

Fettuccini Alfredo is a classic italian dish combining the powerful flavor of Alfredo cheese with the firm starch of a Fettuccini noodle.

The following is my own personal take on it in all its basic necessity. It is a staple meal of mine, and I actually enjoyed it the night I wrote this piece.

Ingredients

  • Package of Fettuccini noodles
  • Jar of Alredo Sauce
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 stick butter

Steps

  1. Prepare a pot of boiling water.
  2. Salt to taste (this means salt until it tastes like your memory of seawater).
  3. Place the noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally. Follow instructions on the pasta package for when to drain.
  4. In a skillet, heat set to medium, cook butter and garlic for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in jar of Alfredo sauce and mix.
  6. Toss Alfredo sauce with pasta.
