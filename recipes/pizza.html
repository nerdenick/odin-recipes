Pizza

Description

Pizza is a favorite of turtles everywhere.

I was originally inspired to start making pizza by a group of turtles dressed as ninjas.

Ingredients

  • Prepackaged Crust
  • Jar of Marinara sauce
  • 1 Package Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Lay crust on heavy baking sheet in a cast iron skillet
  2. Spread Marinara sauce evenly over crust, leaving 1/2 inch exposed along the outside.
  3. Set the oven to 450 degrees.
  4. Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 15 minutes or until crust and cheese are golden brown.
  5. Remove and let sit for 10 minutes, or until cooled.
  6. Enjoy!
